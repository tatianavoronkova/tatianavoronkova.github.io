Logistics Dashboard

Comprehensive visualization tool designed to provide real-time insights and analytics into various aspects of the supply chain and transportation operations. This dashboard offers a holistic view of key performance indicators (KPIs) and metrics essential for strategic decision-making and optimization of logistics processes.

  • Download file

GOAL

The primary aim of the dashboard is to condense critical KPIs and outcomes onto a single page. It aims to provide an interactive platform for evaluating the performance of the logistics process, with a focus on key metrics such as shipment volumes, transit time, carrier status, and country distribution. Additionally, two distinct views are dedicated to examining delivered orders and orders in transit. Reflection of the most crucial KPIs, navigation through the dashboard using the menu on the site, and showcasing significant trends, figures, and numbers are essential features. Through intuitive navigation, users can seamlessly explore the dashboard to gain insights into various aspects of logistics operations and make informed decisions.

KEY FEATURES

  1. Performance Monitoring: This feature provides a comprehensive overview of on-time delivery performance and the current status of shipments in transit. Users can apply various filters to analyze performance over specific date ranges, regions, or carriers, enabling root cause analysis and identification of process weaknesses.
  2. Shipment Tracking: Users can monitor the current status of orders in real-time, enabling proactive measures to prevent shipment delays. By identifying inadequate carrier statuses, potential delivery problems can be addressed promptly, ensuring timely resolution.
  3. Data Detailing: Timeline charts offer flexible grouping options by weeks, months, years, or region, allowing users to drill down from the big picture to individual cases. This granularity facilitates a detailed analysis of logistics data and trends.
  4. Risk Management: The dashboard simplifies the process of identifying insights by providing easy-to-use filters and graphical representations. Users can review different graphs and immediately access relevant information to make informed decisions. It enables the identification of potential risks and disruptions in the supply chain, such as transportation issues or supplier constraints, facilitating proactive risk mitigation strategies.

RESULT

The Logistics Dashboard management provides a centralized platform for monitoring and optimizing logistics operations, ultimately driving efficiency, cost savings, and customer satisfaction across the entire supply chain. With these features, the Logistics Dashboard empowers users to optimize logistics processes, improve performance, and effectively manage risks, ultimately driving greater efficiency and customer satisfaction.
